Strong After 40 - Site Plan

Prepared by Franklin

Site Name

Strong After 40 – This name was selected because it is short, motivational, and represents the mission of promoting health and strength for people over 40. It is easy to remember and brand.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide fitness routines, nutrition tips, and motivational content for adults over 40. It will serve as a hub for practical health advice, resistance band workouts, and lifestyle strategies to stay strong and active.

Scenarios

  • “What simple resistance band exercises can I do at home to stay fit after 40?”
  • “Where can I find nutrition tips and recovery strategies tailored for adults over 40?”

Color Scheme

  • Orange (#e67e22) – Used for accents, buttons, and highlights.
  • Blue (#2a5d84) – Used for headings, navigation bar, and wireframe outlines.
  • Neutral Gray (#333) – Used for body text.

Typography

  • Roboto – Used for headings.
  • Open Sans – Used for body text and paragraphs.

Testing

Before submission, the site plan will be tested using:

  • HTML Validation: W3C Markup Validator
  • Accessibility: WAVE Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool
  • Best Practices, SEO & Performance: Google PageSpeed Insights / Lighthouse
  • Color Contrast: WebAIM Contrast Checker
